The HPLC grade solvents methanol and acetonitrile, used for
extraction and chromatography, respectively, were purchased
from Biosolve (Valkenswaard, The Netherlands). The internal
standard glucotropaeolin was bought from the Laboratory of
Biochemistry, Plant Breeding and Acclimatization Institute at
Radzikow, Blonie, Poland. Sulphatase type H-1, from Helix
pomatia (cat. no. S9626) with an activity of 16 020 U g1 of solids
and DEAE Sephadex A-25 were bought from Sigma-Aldrich.
Milli-pore water was used in the samples undergoing HPLC
analysis. Demineralized water was used to prepare the samples
and tap water was used to cook the pasta for the sensory test.

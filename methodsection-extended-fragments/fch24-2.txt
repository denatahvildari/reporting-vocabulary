\f0\fs24 \cf0 For size exclusion chromatography (SEC), the following instruments were used: the mobile phase\
pump was a Gynkotek N480 pumping an azeotropic mixture of ethyl acetate and cyclohexane (56 : 44, w: w) at a flow of 3 ml/min. The column used was a Pharmacia XK26/40, 26mm i.d., bed height 370 mm, filled with Bio-Beads SX-3 swollen in the eluent described above. The detector was a refraction index detector (Gynkotek RI SE-51), the auto sampler was a Gilson Abimed 231XL equipped with a syringe pump 402. The fraction collector used was a Gilson Abimed 201.\
